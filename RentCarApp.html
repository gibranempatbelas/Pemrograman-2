How a rental flows through GARASI

01

Register the car

Add plate number, model and daily rate to the fleet master. It's saved as Available.

02

Register the customer

Capture ID number, phone and address once — reuse it on every future rental.

03

Open a rental

Pick an available car and a customer, set the dates. The car flips to Rented.

04

Process the return

Log the return date and condition. Late days and damage are billed, the car goes back to Available.
